Airshow Traffic Monitor

One dashboard for watching air traffic in and out of Oshkosh during EAA AirVenture — ATC audio, live YouTube feeds, and FlightRadar24, together.

View on GitHub Suggest a feature

Three feeds, one view

ATC Audio Streams

Listen to tower, approach, and departure streams at once, with independent volume and mute per stream and a visual indicator for which channel is active — so overlapping radio calls stay untangled.

Live YouTube Feeds

Tile EAA's live channels in a grid, emphasize or full-screen any one of them, and pop feeds out into separate windows for a multi-monitor setup.

FlightRadar24 Panel

A fully navigable, resizable browser panel for FlightRadar24, tracking live air traffic alongside the video and audio feeds.

Status

The design is settled and an alpha is being built for AirVenture week: an Electron + TypeScript desktop app, documented in the repository's design docs. Follow along or contribute on GitHub.
